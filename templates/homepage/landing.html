More overview, less redundancy


Graphite is all about saving time by providing smart overviews of unstructured texts. this is realized through a combination of deep learning, semantic analysis and graph theory methods.
Scroll-down to see our three product lines.

Message Scope


Minimize redundancy

For every hot topic Scope News picks exactly one article, namely the one from your favorite sources that it finds to be most representative of all articles on the issue.

Maximize relevance

Reading lists are ordered by the amount of coverage a topic receives in the news overall - meaning you'll always be on top of the news.

Light-weight and quick

Easy to set-up and without installations at your side.

Simplified media curation


Reconsider the way you curate media texts. Message Scope you to curate your sources with ease by only showing the articles which maximise your overview and minimize the redundancy.
