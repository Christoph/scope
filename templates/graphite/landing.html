Reconsider the way you read


NewsButler produces reading lists based on your favourite news sources - curated so as to maximise their relevance and minimise their redundancy.


Minimize redundancy

For every hot topic NewsButler picks exactly one article, namely the one from your favorite sources that it finds to be most representative of all articles on the issue.

Maximize relevance

Reading lists are ordered by the amount of coverage a topic receives in the news overall - meaning you'll always be on top of the news.

Light-weight and discreet

No account, login or fancy interfaces. The lists are sent to your inbox, no more, no less.
