Custom search

Here you can create a graph for your custom search. To do so you have two options: Either you search by search term, or you pick a selection of news feeds. The search will take about 1/4s per article.

In either case, it is important to make clear that the quality of the graph depends massively on the number of input articles! I therefore strongly recommend to make sure that you have at least 250 articles overall. The number of articles in your graph will be shown during once you submit a search.

If your favourite feed is not in the list of options, add it to our database using the form below!
